Tops technology Information

TOPS Technologies, established in 2007 in Ahmedabad, India, is a company specializing in IT training and placement services. It focuses on bridging the gap between educational institutions and industry requirements by offering job-oriented training in areas like software development, web design, and testing. The company also provides placement assistance and recruitment services.

Spinning tops developed independently in cultures all over the world. They are among the oldest recognizable toys found by archaeologists and have been found on every continent except Antarctica. Tops found in China date from around 1250 BCE. A carved wooden top dated to about 2000 BCE was found in King Tut's tomb

Swaminarayan Gurukul

The Shree Swaminarayan Gurukul was founded in 1948 by Sadguru Shastriji Maharaj Shri Dharmajivandasji Swami in Rajkot, Gujarat, India. The inspiration for the Gurukul came from Shastriji Maharaj's pilgrimage to Badrinath, where he felt a calling to preserve and propagate the teachings of Bhagwan Shree Swaminarayan. The Gurukul started with seven students in a rented house and has since grown into a large educational institution with multiple campuses across India and beyond.
